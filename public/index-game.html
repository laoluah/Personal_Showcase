Multi-Sport Arena

A 4-player game featuring Football, American Football, Cricket, and Basketball. Each sport has different mechanics and point values. Test your timing and accuracy to score the most points!

  • Football: Score a goal for 1 point
  • American Football: Kick through the posts for 2 points
  • Cricket: Hit the stump for 3 points
  • Basketball: Score a basket for 4 points
